Preliminary treatment for Vitiligo

  • Topical corticosteroids and calcineurin inhibitors are often used to manage inflammation.
  • Phototherapy, including narrowband UVB and PUVA therapy, can stimulate melanocytes and encourage repigmentation.
  • Depigmentation therapy may be considered for extensive vitiligo to achieve a more uniform skin tone
  • Excimer laser therapy delivers targeted UVB light to depigmented areas, aiding in repigmentation.
