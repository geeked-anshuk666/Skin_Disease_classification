Preliminary treatment for Ringworm

  • Use over-the-counter antifungal creams containing clotrimazole or miconazole.
  • Wash affected areas daily and ensure they are thoroughly dried.
  • Refrain from sharing personal items like towels or clothing.
  • Opt for loose-fitting clothes to keep affected areas cool and dry.


  • We highly recommend you pay a visit to your nearest doctor
