About Us

Skin Types

Dry skin lacks moisture and often feels tight or flaky.

Moisturizer: Cetaphil Daily Hydrating Lotion
Home remedy: Coconut oil or almond oil can be applied to the skin to provide deep hydration and nourishment.

Combination skin has a mix of oily and dry areas on the face.

Moisturizer: Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel
Home remedy: A yogurt and honey mask can help balance the skin's moisture levels. Mix equal parts of plain yogurt and honey, apply to the face, leave on for 15-20 minutes, then rinse off with lukewarm water.

Sensitive skin is easily irritated by external factors.

Moisturizer: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair Face Moisturizer
Home remedy: Aloe vera gel, which has soothing and anti-inflammatory properties, can be applied to calm sensitive skin.

Oily skin produces excess sebum, leading to a shiny appearance.

Moisturizer: Clinique Dramatically Different Moisturizing Gel
Home remedy: A clay mask made with bentonite or kaolin clay can help absorb excess oil and unclog pores. Mix the clay with water or apple cider vinegar to form a paste, apply to the face, leave on until dry, then rinse off with warm water.

Skin Care

Skin care is essential for maintaining healthy and radiant skin. It involves a combination of practices aimed at nourishing, protecting, and rejuvenating the skin. Proper skin care routines typically include cleansing, exfoliating, moisturizing, and applying sunscreen.

Ultimately, investing time and effort into a personalized skin care regimen can lead to healthier, more youthful-looking skin and boost confidence and self-esteem.
